The Secret Beneath The Crystal Surface

Slice-of-Life • Friendship • 308 Pages

Ruby Zhang's summer vacation in Maui starts off normal enough—until she meets a merman on the beach. Back home in foggy San Francisco, Ruby's days are filled with studying for AP exams, late-night band rehearsals, and laughter with her best friends: Grace and Tye, twins with big dreams; Mari, a passionate club leader; and Tomas, a quiet musician.

Together, they are preparing to debut their first original song at the end-of-year variety show. Yet beneath the surface of ordinary high school days, among these close friends, there are still unspoken fears and quiet hopes: the pressure to be perfect, the weight of family expectations, the longing for something—or someone—just out of reach.

When Ruby misses the show as her family takes her to Hawaii for her dad's business trip, Ruby's dismay quickly fades when she meets someone impossible: a merman beneath the waves. A gentle coming-of-age story about friendship, music, and the secrets we keep, following five friends as they finish their junior year of high school and into summer.
