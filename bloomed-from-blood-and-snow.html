She Who Bloomed From Blood And Snow

Fantasy • Vampires • 280 Pages

She Who Bloomed From Blood And Snow is the story of a young woman with many names trying to find her way after being turned into a vampire. She was days away from her long-anticipated marriage with her childhood sweetheart—until a vampire tore her world apart.

Forced into a new world of darkness, she clings to memories of love, warmth, and the life she lost. Even in her darkest hours, instead of breaking, she blooms into something powerful, something dangerous, something even she never expected.

But upon becoming immortal, all she can do is watch time and time again as the people and lands she loves wither around her. She fights to remain on the right path, but in a world of eternal night, it is easy to get lost in the darkness.
