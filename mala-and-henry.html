Mala & Henry: 100 Days In The Overworld

Middle Grade • Adventure

Two students. One creeper bookmark. A whole world turned upside down.

When brave and curious Mala and bookish Henry discover a strange creeper bookmark, they're suddenly pulled right into the blocky world of Minecraft! Both Minecraft players themselves, they must relearn how to punch trees, craft tools, and fight monsters. Inside the game, everything is different than on screen: it's real, and so are the dangers.

At Cherry Wood Academy, a giant base and school built by other trapped kids like themselves, Mala and Henry make new friends and attend classes to learn how to live and adapt to their new life in Minecraft. Rumor has it that a portal home appears every hundred days, and they just need to figure out how to activate it when it does.

The Stakes

Not everyone wants to go home

But monsters aren't the only ones Mala and Henry need to worry about while they wait. Griefers, a group of runaways from Cherry Wood Academy, are fed up with being stuck in the game and have decided to find an exit on their own — at all costs.

Mala, Henry, and their new friends are determined to get everyone home. This means uniting the kids of Cherry Wood Academy and the griefers, discovering along the way that there is strength in numbers, and that the best way out is together.
