Middle Grade

Mala & Henry: 100 Days In The Overworld

Adventure • Middle Grade

When brave and curious Mala and bookish Henry discover a strange creeper bookmark, they're suddenly pulled right into the blocky world of Minecraft! At Cherry Wood Academy, a giant base and school built by other trapped kids, they must relearn how to punch trees, craft tools, and fight monsters — while a rumor of a portal home appears every hundred days. Mala, Henry, and their new friends must unite trapped kids and rebellious griefers alike, discovering that the best way out is together.

The Four Gods of Hali

Fantasy • Adventure • 318 Pages

In the magical world of Hali, Halimah Samra is a focused student who tries to ignore her stuck-up competitor, Ewen Hanif — until her magic suddenly changes and she discovers she is the child of the late Goddess of Life. Her old rival becomes her closest ally as they travel under the guidance of the cat Professor Lethco to seek the wisdom of the remaining former gods, only to find the gods are vanishing. Halimah stands at the crossroads of fate, friendship, and the discovery of her true self.

Ruby Zhang's summer vacation in Maui starts off normal enough — until she meets a merman on the beach.
