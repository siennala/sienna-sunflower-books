The Author

Hi, I'm Sienna

I draw inspiration for my stories from fairies, mermaids, and dragons. With a pen in hand, I've traveled through magical worlds, gone on exciting adventures, and made the best of friends.

Whether it's a school built inside Minecraft, a kingdom of vanishing gods, or a demon longing to be human, every world I write begins with the same question: what if magic were real, right here, in the middle of an ordinary day?

I write for readers who still believe that friendship can move mountains, that courage comes in small moments, and that there's always a little wonder hiding just beneath the surface.

— Sienna Sunflower
