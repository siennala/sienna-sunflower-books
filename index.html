Mala & Henry

When brave and curious Mala and bookish Henry discover a strange creeper bookmark, they're suddenly pulled right into the blocky world of Minecraft! At Cherry Wood Academy, a giant base and school built by other trapped kids, they must relearn how to punch trees, craft tools, and fight monsters — while a rumor of a portal home appears every hundred days.
