The Four Gods Of Hali

In the magical world of Hali, Halimah Samra is a focused student attending the top magic academy and tries her best to ignore her stuck-up competitor, Ewen Hanif. But when Halimah's magic suddenly changes, her world is turned upside down as she discovers she is the child of the late Goddess of Life. Her old rival becomes her closest ally, and Halimah is swept into a journey across Hali. She and Ewen travel under the guidance of the cat Professor Lethco to seek the wisdom of the remaining former gods, only to discover the former gods are vanishing.
