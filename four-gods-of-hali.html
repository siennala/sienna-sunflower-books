The Four Gods Of Hali

Fantasy • Adventure • 318 Pages

In the magical world of Hali, Halimah Samra is a focused student attending the top magic academy and tries her best to ignore her stuck-up competitor, Ewen Hanif. But when Halimah's magic suddenly changes, her world is turned upside down as she discovers she is the child of the late Goddess of Life.

Her old rival becomes her closest ally, and Halimah is swept into a journey across Hali. She and Ewen travel under the guidance of the cat Professor Lethco to seek the wisdom of the remaining former gods, only to discover the former gods are vanishing.

Halimah must learn to master her newfound magic, and as whispers of war stir between the winged folk, her journey leads her to Saedyn, the son of the former flame god, and Crow, a mail carrier with a hidden gift: the rare and dangerous void magic. Halimah stands at the crossroads of fate, friendship, and the discovery of her true self in this sweeping tale of magic, destiny, and the courage to choose your own path.
