To Sail The Sea Of Stars

Fantasy • Vampires • 548 Pages

Corinna has only ever known the cold cruelty of slavery, where each day is a slow torture under the rule of a kingdom that has no place for her people. But everything changes the night she saves a stranger in the woods and discovers that the woman is a vampire.

Talon is an immortal burdened by blood and regret. Though, despite being a vampire, her mind, emotions, and unbeating heart are very much human. When Corinna risks her life for her, a fragile trust begins to form between them.

As winter deepens, Corinna's younger sister is torn away from the rest of her family. Corinna must decide if she's willing to risk everything to reunite with her, even if it means defying the chains of her world and allying herself with vampires to reach for something greater. As she and Talon continue to grow closer, Corinna has to balance her family's concerns about her new vampire companion with following her own heart and conscience.
